About AARRT Barbershop

AARRT Barbershop is the fulfillment of a lifelong dream. Anhel has been cutting hair since the age of 12. From the moment he received a gift from his brother in the form of a silver-colored Oster trimming machine, he knew that owning his personal barbershop would be his goal.

At AARRT Barbershop, Anhel caters to customers of all ages, from infants to seniors. His personal motto is, “I am only happy if you are happy with your trim.” Situated in the quaint village of Ontario in the Cayo District, AARRT Barbershop offers a range of services from traditional scissor cuts to modern machine cuts.

Whether you're looking for a classic or contemporary style, Anhel will ensure that you leave feeling confident and satisfied with your haircut.
